Jira reference: 1420 Change claim start date

An effort to allow people to change their claim start date at the end of their application without losing all of the information from their application.

What we don't know is why when a customer changes the claim date the data is not persisted on some parts of the application.

Worked on by: Neil Holroyd, Gareth Joyce, Mark Bateman
