Jira reference: 1401 Improve eligibility questions

Effort to improve the vagueness of eligibility for JSA to move away from might be/might not be eligible. Also looks at improvements to NI contributions questions. DWP Confluence page.

Worked on by: Anne Ramsden, Gareth Joyce, Dan Howarth, Garrett Stettler

Versions

  • 20 October 2020

    #3 - Gareth's hypothesis

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get New Style JSA.

    So if we provide clear information when we know they are or aren't eligible and signposting for users to get support to find this out.

    We’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.

  • 9 October 2020

    #2 - Dan's hypothesis

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get New Style JSA.

    So if we provide clear information about NI contributions and how the user will know if they’ve paid them.

    We’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.

  • 9 October 2020

    #1 - Anne's hypothesis

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get New Style JSA.

    So if we ask specific questions about employment and benefits - rather than about NI contributions - and route the user accordingly.

    We’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.
