Jira reference: 1401 Improve eligibility questions

Effort to improve the vagueness of eligibility for JSA to move away from might be/might not be eligible. Also looks at improvements to NI contributions questions. DWP Confluence page.

Worked on by: Anne Ramsden, Gareth Joyce, Dan Howarth, Garrett Stettler

    #5 Focus on past 2 to 3 years only

    {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "Rita’s hypothesis", html: '

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get New Style JSA;

    so if we make it clear we only care about the last 2 to 3 years;

    we’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.

    ' }) }}

    #4 Telling users we check

    {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "Gareth’s hypothesis", html: '

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get New Style JSA;

    so if we tell them that we will check their eligibility, we make them check before they apply;

    we’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.

    ' }) }}

    #3 Removing doubt and self serving

    {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "Gareth’s hypothesis", html: '
