Jira reference: 1401 Improve eligibility questions

Effort to improve the vagueness of eligibility for JSA to move away from might be/might not be eligible. Also looks at improvements to NI contributions questions. DWP Confluence page.

Worked on by: Anne Ramsden, Gareth Joyce, Dan Howarth, Garrett Stettler

    #3 Removing doubt and self serving

    {{ govukDetails({ summaryText: "Gareth’s hypotheses", html: '

    We’ve seen people being told they are not eligible to get Jobseeker’s allowance after applying;

    so if we provide clear information when we know they are or aren’t eligible;

    we’ll see fewer applications rejected.

    We’ve seen people wrongly saying they’ve made enough National Insurance contributions to get Jobseeker’s allowance;

    so if we provide clear signposting to help users find this out for themselves;

    we’ll see fewer applications rejected on the basis of NI contributions.
